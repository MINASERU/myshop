About US



CONCEPT
Music, screens, projections, modern graphics, fashion, colors...

Bershka stores stand out in the world with their strategic location and well-detailed architecture. The stores determine the fashion with their showcases, which are the windows of the products offered inside. Bershka selects the best trade centers and locations in each city and is located in major shopping centers. Detached buildings are preferred and Bershka architectural studio designs are studied according to the brand's image and philosophy.

















OUR PRODUCTS

BSK is the brand of young people. Therefore, the products are always geared towards the needs and interests of young people.

Products belonging to different lines under Bershka are presented in different areas in stores. In each section, Bershka, BSK&Man products are classified and presented according to their styles. The stores have all kinds of products, from casual wear to sportswear and from basics to the latest trends of the season.

BRAND

BRAND

Bershka describes itself as a brand focused on increasing demand. In just 2 years, it strengthens its brand image in 100 stores. Currently, the 18-year-old chain of stores has more than 1,000 stores in more than 70 markets, with sales representing 9% of total revenue across the group. Bershkan's target audience is adventurous young people who follow the latest trends in music, social media and new technologies.



Growth and expansion through integrated channels

H&M Group is expanding online, with a focus on omnichannel sales. Our customers want to be able to shop and be inspired when and how they chose – in our stores, on our brands’ own websites, on digital marketplaces and in social media.
